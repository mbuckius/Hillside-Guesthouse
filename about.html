About

A Hill Country hideaway!

An exclusive and modern cabin/barn tucked 3/4 mile from Ranch Road 1631 amidst oak trees. A true sanctuary for nature lovers and for those who want to escape city life to enjoy the stillness of country life. Nothing like a long-day-excursion from wine tasting, shopping and dining in town to end the day in a zen-like space and to wake up to the sight and sounds of wildlife.

We offer a 1,500 square foot 1 BR 2 BA, only 12.6 miles from Main Street. The guesthouse has a large full kitchen, separate dining area adjacent to a screen-enclosed deck overlooking breathtaking sweeping views of Hill Country. The space is modern, simply decorated, and uncluttered to offer you a bright and airy ambience for de-stressing and soul-soothing peace of mind and body.
